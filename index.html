Weidong Huang

Reinforcement Learning & Robotics · Beihang University · Visiting Student @ BIGAI Robotics Lab

Shenzhen & Beijing, China

bigeasthuang@gmail.com

GitHub: hdadong

I am Weidong Huang, a master’s student at Beihang University and a visiting research engineer at the Robotics Lab of the Beijing Institute of General Artificial Intelligence (BIGAI) in Shenzhen. My work centers on reinforcement learning for robotics—world models, safe control, and imitation learning for humanoid locomotion.

Currently, I build sim-to-real pipelines that allow humanoid robots to walk efficiently with low energy consumption. Before BIGAI, I interned at Tencent AI Lab (Honor of Kings imitation learning) and Peking University (safe RL with world models).

Recent projects include:

  • Lift Humanoid (ICLR’26 under review): Large-scale SAC pretraining plus physics-informed world-model finetuning for humanoid control.
  • ECO: Energy-constrained RL with gait-symmetry constraints for energy-efficient humanoid walking.
  • SafeDreamer (ICLR’24 poster): World-model-based constrained policy optimization for safe RL.
  • OmniSafe: Led the model-based RL module, contributing 13k+ LOC to the SafeRL library.

My research interests: Reinforcement Learning, World Models, Humanoid & Robot Learning, Imitation Learning, Safe RL.
